### Key Context from the Sources * **The Jellyman's Reports:** These files discovered within SERN's servers mention "Human is dead, mismatch," implying a history of failed human experimentation. * **The IBN 5100's Role:** John Titor informs Okabe that this specific retro-computer is required to decode SERN’s proprietary protocols and stop their conspiracy,. * **The Shrine Connection:** Faris provides the lead that the computer was at a shrine. Luka Urushibara’s father, Eisuke, confirms it was left there ten years ago with a prophecy that a young man would eventually come to claim it. * **The Retrieval:** Despite Okabe's "G-Back" strategy failing in Rai-Net, he obtains the information needed. He and Kurisu eventually carry the heavy machine back to the lab together.
