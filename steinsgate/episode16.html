Key Conceptual Analysis from the Sources

  • Barrel Titor Identified: Mayuri deduces that "Barrel" translates to "Taru" in Japanese, revealing that "Barrel Titor" is actually **Itaru Hashida** (Daru). This is confirmed by the time machine's name, FG204, which stands for "Future Gadget No. 204".
  • The Two Timelines of 1975:
    • First Worldline (Failure): Okabe stopped Suzuha from leaving during a storm, causing the time machine to break. In 1975, Suzuha suffered from amnesia for 24 years, only remembering her mission a year before her suicide in 2000.
    • Second Worldline (Success): Okabe sends a D-mail to his past self to *not* stop Suzuha. In this timeline, the machine isn't damaged, Suzuha fulfills her life in the past, dies peacefully of illness, and inspires Mr. Braun.
  • Divergence Barrier: Although the worldline shifts and the "bomb threat" (used by SERN to stop trains) is averted, the Divergence Meter shows they are still below the 1% threshold, leaving Mayuri's ultimate fate uncertain.
