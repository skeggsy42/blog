Saturday, June 24th, 2006

Appointment with Magnet designer/salesman in Orpington.
Showroom was very slick, lots of plasma screens which is nice- obviously Magnet are doing well. Bit early so browsed around. Almost hit in the head by a piece of wood that flew out of a high cabinet - it did have a sticker on it saying open me so bit sloppy on the health and safety side! Designer/salesman was very pleasant, that is until the end when I said our timescale was 1 year and then he was suddenly very off. That was rather disappointing really. I asked how long they save the quote for and he said they save the quote for one week and that they don’t work to 1 year timescales! He obviously cussed us to one of the other salesmen who meandered round to give us a quick look. Considering how glossy the showroom looks I expected a bit more professionalism but I guess they don’t need our money. Anyway the quote was midway at �5300 after discounts which is ok. Kitchen

Sunday, June 11th, 2006

Had moben designer in for a quote (http://www.moben.co.uk). He measured up and drew a scale plan of our kitchen. He had some good ideas on the layout of our kitchen which we hadn’t thought of. He even left a copy of the plan. Only concern is the price included a 20% discount which you only get if you take their finance agreement. You can pay the finance company within the first month without incurring any interest charges but this is effectively paying up front before the work is done. Otherwise we really like the red kitchen!
Also visited porcelanosa to get prices for floor tiles.
Considering other options, like idea of importing our own kitchen from Germany!
