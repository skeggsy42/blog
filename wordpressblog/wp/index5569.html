Egypt. El Gouna.

Monday, March 8th, 2010

One day boat trip with Orca Diving, next to Turtle Inn on Marina. Cost 453LE.
Very pleasant boat to spend a day on, sunbathing, reading and snorkelling. Lunch served on board. Some great snorkelling near Sinai. Second stopoff was not so good for snorkellers, probably better for the divers.
